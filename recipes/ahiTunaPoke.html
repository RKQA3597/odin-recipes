Ahi Tuna Poke

Description

As a woman with a full time job and doing schooling on the side, I don't often get time to cook. However, my husband is Hawaiian and a great cook. So this recipe is for him!

This easy poke recipe is a refreshing Hawaiian salad of fresh ahi tuna steak cubes tossed with soy sauce, sesame oil, and green onions for a dish full of umami flavor. I like to add chopped macadamia nuts even though they are not authentic â€” they add a delicious crunch! This makes 4 main course servings or 8 appetizer servings.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh tuna cubed
  • 1/2 cup soy sauce
  • 3/4 cup chopped green onion
  • 1 Tablespoons sesame oil
  • 1 Tablespoon finely chopped macadamia nuts
  • 1 Tablespoon sesame seeds
  • optional:1 Tablespoon crushed red pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Add all ingredients to a large bowl.
  3. Stir together all ingredients until all ingredients are thoroughly mixed.
  4. Serve over your favorite side such as rice, lettuce, tortilla chips or eat it plain!
